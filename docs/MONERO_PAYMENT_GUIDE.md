## üîß **DETAILED INSTRUCTIONS**

### **Getting Monero:**

#### **Via Exchange (Kraken - Recommended):**

1. **Sign Up:**
   - Go to https://www.kraken.com
   - Create account
   - Verify email

2. **Verify Identity:**
   - Complete KYC (Know Your Customer)
   - Upload ID
   - Wait for approval (usually 1-2 days)

3. **Deposit Funds:**
   - Go to "Funding" ‚Üí "Deposit"
   - Choose deposit method (bank transfer, credit card)
   - Send funds

4. **Buy Monero:**
   - Go to "Trade" ‚Üí "Monero (XMR)"
   - Buy XMR with your deposited funds
   - You now have Monero!

5. **Withdraw to Wallet:**
   - Go to "Funding" ‚Üí "Withdraw"
   - Select Monero (XMR)
   - Enter your wallet address
   - Enter amount
   - Confirm withdrawal

#### **Via P2P Exchange (LocalMonero - More Private):**

1. **Visit LocalMonero.co**
2. **Create account** (no KYC required for small amounts)
3. **Search for sellers** in your area
4. **Choose payment method** (cash, bank transfer, etc.)
5. **Complete trade** (follow escrow process)
6. **Receive Monero** in your wallet

### **Setting Up Wallet:**

#### **Official GUI Wallet (Desktop - Recommended for Large Amounts):**

1. **Download:**
   - Visit https://www.getmonero.org/downloads/
   - Download for your OS (Windows/Mac/Linux)
   - Install

2. **First Time Setup:**
   - Open wallet
   - Choose "Create new wallet"
   - **Save seed phrase** (25 words - this is critical!)
   - Set password
   - Wait for blockchain sync (can take hours first time)

3. **Receiving Monero:**
   - Click "Receive"
   - Copy your address
   - Share with sender
   - Monero will appear after confirmation

4. **Sending Monero:**
   - Click "Send"
   - Paste recipient address
   - Enter amount
   - Confirm transaction

#### **Cake Wallet (Mobile - Easier for Beginners):**

1. **Download:**
   - iOS: App Store
   - Android: Google Play
   - Search "Cake Wallet"

2. **Create Wallet:**
   - Open app
   - Tap "Create New Wallet"
   - **Save seed phrase** (12 words)
   - Set PIN

3. **Using Wallet:**
   - Receiving: Tap "Receive" ‚Üí Copy address
   - Sending: Tap "Send" ‚Üí Paste address ‚Üí Enter amount

### **Sending Payment:**

1. **Get Payment Details:**
   - You'll receive payment page link after application approval
   - Page shows:
     - Amount in USD: $10,000
     - Amount in XMR: [calculated amount]
     - Monero address: [long address starting with 4...]

2. **Open Your Wallet:**
   - Open Monero wallet
   - Make sure it's synced (shows balance correctly)

3. **Send Payment:**
   - Click/tap "Send"
   - Paste the Monero address from payment page
   - **Enter the EXACT amount** shown (in XMR, not USD)
   - Double-check address and amount
   - Confirm transaction

4. **Payment ID (if required):**
   - Some wallets ask for Payment ID
   - If shown on payment page, include it
   - Otherwise, leave blank or use integrated address

5. **Wait for Confirmation:**
   - Transaction will show as "Pending" in wallet
   - Confirms within 15 minutes (usually faster)
   - You'll receive email when payment is confirmed
